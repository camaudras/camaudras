Santa Barbara Viola Lessons

Private viola instruction in downtown Santa Barbara for beginning through advanced students.

I offer thoughtful, individualized lessons designed to help students develop a healthy technique, a rich and confident sound, strong musicianship, and a lasting enjoyment of music. As a viola faculty member at Westmont College and an active professional violist, I bring specialized knowledge of the instrument to students at every stage.

Who I Teach

I welcome children, teens, and adults, including students beginning the viola for the first time and experienced players seeking more focused technical or musical guidance.

Beginning Violists

A supportive introduction to setup, tone, rhythm, note-reading, and productive practice habits.

Youth Orchestra Students

Preparation for auditions, seating, orchestra repertoire, sight-reading, and confident ensemble playing.

Advancing Students

Detailed work on solo repertoire, technique, excerpts, performance preparation, and college or festival auditions.

Violinists Transitioning to Viola

Specialized help with alto clef, instrument setup, tone production, bow weight, and the distinct musical role of the viola.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe every student learns differently, and I strive to create a lesson environment that is supportive, focused, and appropriately challenging. My goal is to help students build a strong technical foundation while also developing confidence, musical curiosity, and an expressive personal voice.

Lessons emphasize a healthy instrument-body relationship, including comfortable posture and setup, efficient movement, resonant tone production, reliable intonation, rhythm, and practical practice strategies. I want students not only to improve, but to understand how to improve so they can become thoughtful and increasingly independent musicians.

Whether a student is preparing for youth orchestra, developing solo repertoire, learning the basics for the first time, or returning to music as an adult, I aim to meet them where they are and help them grow steadily and joyfully.

What Students Can Work Toward

  • A comfortable and sustainable playing setup
  • A fuller, more resonant viola sound
  • Improved intonation, rhythm, and bow control
  • More effective and independent practice habits
  • Greater confidence in auditions and performances
  • Preparation for school, youth orchestra, festival, or college auditions
  • Growth through solo, chamber, and orchestral repertoire
